Stealing

Stealing a ball from the player is one of the hardest and beneficial in the game. Stealing is hard because you might end up
fouling the player if you don't time the moment right. Fouling the player will give them a chance to have a more organized
plan. So the way you should try to steal the ball is when the player is agressive and tries to make the point.
You should also be cautious and predict the most possiblee moves the player can make, so you have the right timing and spot
for stealing the ball.
